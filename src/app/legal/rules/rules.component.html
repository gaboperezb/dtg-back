Posting Rules

  1. The context of the post must be related to the chosen sport.
  2. For news, posts that discuss issues raised in previous posts may be removed.
  3. Posts must not be fake.
  4. Posts must be original creations and not copyrighted content.
  5. The content of the post must be relevant.
  6. No spam.
  7. It is forbidden to upload links related to streamings.
  8. If your post makes it to the hot or top section, priority will be given to the one that was uploaded before.
  9. The title, description, and if necessary, poll options, must not be in capitals.
  10. The post must not be offensive.
  11. The post must be well written and of high quality.
  12. You cannot promote any brand or social media page on the post.
